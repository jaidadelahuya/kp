Career Cluster

Career Cluster Test

Everyone fits in somewhere. You too. It's high time you discovered where you fit in the global economy so you can pursue fulfilling careers. Every career falls into one of 16 "Career Clusters" and inside your DNA are clusters of careers that you can pursue and excel at.

A career cluster is a group of industries and jobs that are related by the skills applied or products manufactured in those industries. There are 16 of them. And in each of them there are career pathways. Let's find out the clusters you fit in.

Instructions

Career Values

Career Values

In order for you to pursue fulfilling, interesting careers, you must first know what type of work actually interests you, and what kind of work environment suits you.

If your career choices do not agree with your values, you'd struggle in it. Therefore it pays to identify your values before heading out the door in pursuit of career success. Knowing and understanding what's important to you about work will help you to choose an occupation and a work environment that matches your values.

Choose skills to build

Skill builder

When you develop your talents into skills then you can earn an income! For example someone gifted in cooking can develop a skill as a chef or caterer, another person gifted in running can become a sprinter (an athlete). What skills can you develop out of your talents? This test helps you learn about the skills or jobs you can pursue based on your talents. With it you can create a plan of study for your skills acquisition and your dream job.

Instructions

The robotics in the app will extract your talents from your profile and display for you. Take time to look at the different skills/jobs listed under each of your talents and select the ones you'd love to start learning. The skills you select to learn would be saved in your profile. Those skills you've already developed will be saved in your profile for potential for potential employers to see. This plan would serve as your virtual guide.
